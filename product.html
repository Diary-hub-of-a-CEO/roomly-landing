How RoomLy works.

A unified platform that connects tenants, landlords, and local housing services with clarity and trust.

Verified listings

Each room is reviewed, verified, and matched to tenant expectations before it appears in search.

Smart search

Filters for location, budget, roommates, safety, and move-in date help local renters find the right match quickly.

Tenant onboarding

Fast form-based leads, landlord messaging, and verified contact details reduce back-and-forth and speed up decisions.

Landlord dashboard

Landlords get a clean view of listing performance, tenant requests, and verified applications in one place.
