Frequently asked questions.

Everything you need to know about RoomLy.

What is RoomLy?
RoomLy is Nepal’s new housing platform for tenants and landlords. Tenants can discover verified rooms, flats, hostels, and co-living spaces in one place. Landlords can list properties, manage inquiries, and reach trusted renters faster.
When can I use RoomLy?
Early access is open now, and the platform will roll out progressively in 2026. Join the waitlist today to get the first invite, priority updates, and access to new features as soon as they launch.
Is RoomLy free to use?
Yes, browsing and searching are free for tenants. Landlords get free basic listings and can upgrade later for premium placement and landlord tools once the platform is live.
Can landlords list properties now?
Yes. Landlords can sign up as founding partners and add property details today. We verify listings to keep the marketplace reliable and help you connect with serious tenants.
How do I become a founding partner?
Complete the form in the For Landlords section and share your property info. Our team will contact you with partner benefits, onboarding support, and next steps for early launch promotion.
Will RoomLy serve all cities in Nepal?
Yes. We are launching in major cities first and expanding quickly across Nepal. Our goal is to cover Kathmandu, Lalitpur, Bhaktapur, Pokhara, and other key regions soon after launch.
