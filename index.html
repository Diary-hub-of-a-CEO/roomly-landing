Frequently asked questions.

Everything you need to know about RoomLy.

What is RoomLy?
RoomLy is Nepal's upcoming smart housing platform designed to centralize verified listings, connect tenants with landlords, and make the rental process seamless.
When is RoomLy launching?
We're launching soon in 2026. Early access is already available for interested users and property partners.
Is RoomLy free?
Yes, RoomLy is free for tenants to browse and book properties. Landlords also get free listing and management tools.
Can landlords list properties?
Absolutely! We're actively recruiting founding partners. Join our program to gain early visibility and priority support.
How do I become a founding partner?
Fill out the "Become a Founding Partner" form in the For Landlords section. We'll get in touch with limited spots available.
Will RoomLy be available across Nepal?
Yes, our vision is to expand across Nepal and eventually become the go-to housing platform nationwide.
